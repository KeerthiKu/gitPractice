How to travel to paris on a budget

Do you want to travel to paris on a budget? then keep reading!

Paris the city of love, is a beautiful place to experience and enjoy. paris is also the capital of france.

Follow these steps:

  • Don't take taxes for short distance walk or take public transport because it will cost you less money then you taking a taxi
  • 2. take a bus from the airport other than a taxi
  • 3. consider taking a Paris visitor card for traveling on the bus
  • 4. save some money by bike riding to visit close bye destinations
  • 5. a simple €1 or €2 is perfectly fine for a tip at a restaurant don't need to pay more.
  • 6. breakfast served in a hotel is very underwhelming and overpriced so try not to order food in a hotel and eat at a nearby restaurant
  • 7. don't use the pone that is in the hotel room because if you call from it that will cost you a lot of money.
  • 8. water and bread are free in restaurants so don't buy bottles of water.
